Script Programming

At one of my previous jobs, we had to design solar panel systems for residential rooftops. Google sketchup was our 3D modeling software.

Using a combination of Ruby and Google Sketchup's API, I was able to write scripts for the team that would speed up the process immensely. Rather than draw individual parts of the home, using my script, we could enter the relevant dimensions, and automate the generation of the roof.

Songwriting

Writing and recording music has always been more gratifying to me than learning how to play other people's music. I've been writing music for 18 years and recording for 14 respectively. My education consists of a B.A. in Music and Sound Recording and an Associates degree in Broadcasting.

Sound for Film

Being able to write music for film has been a great extension of my musical interests. It allows me to explore sounds and ideas that exist outside of my personal songwriting. I've been working on music for films, podcasts, tv-shows, and radio since 2008.

Sound design and mixing for media use the skills that I've picked up through years of sitting in front of speakers with a computer. Recently, I did the sound design and mixing for a film about rollerblading; One For the Road
